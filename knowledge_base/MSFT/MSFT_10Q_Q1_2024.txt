## Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

**Overview:**
For the first quarter of fiscal year 2024, ended September 30, 2023, Microsoft reported revenue of $55.7 billion, an increase of 9% (8% in constant currency) compared to the same period in the prior year. This growth was primarily driven by continued momentum in our Intelligent Cloud segment, particularly Azure, and solid performance in Productivity and Business Processes. Net income was $19.9 billion, an increase of 8% year-over-year.

**Segment Performance (Q1 FY2024 vs Q1 FY2023):**
- **Productivity and Business Processes:** Revenue of $18.6 billion, up 13% (12% in constant currency). Office 365 Commercial revenue grew 15% (14% in constant currency). LinkedIn revenue increased 8%.
- **Intelligent Cloud:** Revenue of $24.3 billion, up 19% (19% in constant currency). Azure and other cloud services revenue grew 29% (28% in constant currency), driven by strong demand for our AI services.
- **More Personal Computing:** Revenue of $12.8 billion, down 3% (down 3% in constant currency). Windows OEM revenue declined 4%, while Xbox content and services revenue grew 13%.
