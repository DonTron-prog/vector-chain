## Item 2. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

**Overview:**
For the third quarter of fiscal 2023, Apple reported net sales of $81.8 billion, a decrease of 1% compared to the same period in the prior year. This was primarily due to foreign exchange headwinds and a challenging macroeconomic environment impacting product sales. Services revenue continued its strong growth trajectory. Net income for the quarter was $20.5 billion, an increase of 5% year-over-year.

**Segment Performance (Q3 2023 vs Q3 2022):**
- **iPhone:** Net sales of $39.7 billion, down 2%.
- **Mac:** Net sales of $6.8 billion, down 7%.
- **iPad:** Net sales of $5.8 billion, down 20%.
- **Wearables, Home and Accessories:** Net sales of $8.3 billion, up 2%.
- **Services:** Net sales of $21.2 billion, up 8%.
