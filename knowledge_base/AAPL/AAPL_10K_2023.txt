# Apple Inc. - Form 10-K - Fiscal Year Ended September 30, 2023

## Item 1. Business

Apple Inc. designs, manufactures, and markets smartphones (iPhone), personal computers (Mac), tablets (iPad), wearables (Apple Watch, AirPods), and accessories worldwide. It also sells various related services, including the App Store, Apple Music, Apple TV+, iCloud, and AppleCare. The Company sells its products and resells third-party products in most of its major markets directly to consumers, small and mid-sized businesses and education, enterprise and government customers through its retail and online stores and its direct sales force. The Company also sells its products through third-party cellular network carriers, wholesalers, retailers and resellers.

## Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

**Overview:**
For the fiscal year ended September 30, 2023, Apple reported net sales of $383.3 billion, a decrease of 3% compared to $394.3 billion in the prior year. This decrease was primarily driven by lower iPhone and Mac sales, partially offset by growth in Services revenue. Net income for fiscal 2023 was $97.0 billion, or $6.13 per diluted share, compared to $99.8 billion, or $6.11 per diluted share, in fiscal 2022.

**Segment Performance:**
- **iPhone:** Net sales of $200.6 billion, down 2% year-over-year.
- **Mac:** Net sales of $29.4 billion, down 27% year-over-year.
- **iPad:** Net sales of $28.3 billion, down 3% year-over-year.
- **Wearables, Home and Accessories:** Net sales of $39.8 billion, down 3% year-over-year.
- **Services:** Net sales of $85.2 billion, up 9% year-over-year, driven by growth in advertising, App Store, and cloud services.

**Liquidity and Capital Resources:**
As of September 30, 2023, the Company had $61.5 billion in cash and cash equivalents and $109.8 billion in marketable securities. The Company continues to return capital to shareholders through dividends and share repurchases.
